  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 5-6 minutes until pliable, then drain.
  2. Marinate beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, cornstarch, and 1 tbsp oil for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok over high heat.
  4. Quickly stir-fry beef until 70% cooked (about 1 minute), then remove and set aside.
  5. Add remaining oil, then toss in garlic chives and bean sprouts for 30 seconds.
  6. Add noodles, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Stir-fry evenly.
  7. Return beef to the wok, toss well, and cook for another minute.
